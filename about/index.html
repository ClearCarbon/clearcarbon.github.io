About

My name is Chris Hunt, I live in Sheffield, UK with my wonderful wife and daughter. I’m a Software Engineer and Senior Developer at epiGenesys.

I’m regularly found at a number of user groups around Sheffield including First Play, Shrug, Sheffield.js. I organise Geeks in the Pub, a monthly meetup for all geeks, with @richardathome and @davidjmemmett.

A lot of my spare time is put into running an online gaming community called HoG. We organise regular LAN Parties which people attend from across the country and often from Europe.
